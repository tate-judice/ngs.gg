Passive Skills

HP

Overload Endurance

During Overload, an incapacitating attack will leave you with 1 HP and end Overload. You also gain a moment of invulnerability when this activates.

PP

Overload PP Recovery

Increases natural PP recovery and PP recovery when attacking during Overload.

DMG

Overload Short Cycle

Reduces the effect duration of Overload and increases the damage you take, but shortens its cooldown time.

PP

Overload After PP Preservation

Decreases PP consumption for a set time after Overload effect ends. Does not activate if ended by Overload Endurance's effect.

DMG

Dagger Spin Axle

At just the right timing after performing a weapon action, you can perform an Acceleration Drive using the normal attack button.

PP

Saber Glaze PP Gain

You become able to recover PP when you hit with stage two of whirlwind.

PP

Knuckle Arts Combo PP Save

Reduces PP consumption in accordance with the number of photon arts you chain together.

PP

Knuckle Arts Combo Bonus

Further Improves the decreased PP consumption rate of Knuckles Arts Combo PP Saver, when you unleash the first half of a Photon Art.

Active Skills

DMG

Overload

Increases the damage you take for a set time in exchange for increased weapon potency.
