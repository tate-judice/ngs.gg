Passive Skills

PP

Defeat PP Gain

You recover PP when a nearby enemy is downed.

DMG

Physical Decline

Hitting an enemy with your Photon Arts makes it easier to induce Physical Down. An additional PA hit can increase the Down Factor up to Lv. 3.

DMG

Elemental Decline

Hitting an enemy with your Photon Arts makes it easier to induce Elemental Down. An additional PA hit can increase the Down Factor up to Lv. 3.

DMG

Decline Amplifier

Down Factor Increases stages will increase more easily for Physical Decline and Elemental Decline.

DMG

Decline Jellen Effect

Adds a Weaken Enemy Attack effect to Physical Decline and Elemental Decline. The effect is increased if you afflict the enemy with both Physical and Elemental Decline.

DMG

Special Ability Optimization BO

The Melee Weapon Potency and Technique Weapon Potency bonuses from your equipped weapon and units Augments are each added up, and the one that is higher is applied to both your Melee and Technique Weapon Potency.

HP

Blade Arts Parry

Enemy attacks are nullified just once when you press the weapon action button while unleashing a Photon Art.

DMG

Blade Arts Parry Counter Edge

Unleash a counter when you successfully negate an attack with Blade Arts Parry.

DMG

Jetsweep Jolt Rapid Boost

Increases your Photon Art attack speed for a set time when you use Jetsweep Jolt.

PP

Jetsweep Jolt PP Save

Your PP consumption is temporarily reduced after using Jetsweep Jolt.

DMG

Jetsweep Jolt Short Cooldown

When you successfully dodge with a Weapon Action, the cooldown time for Jetsweep Jolt is reduced.

Active Skills

DMG

Fanatic Blade

Increases the frequency and potency of your Photon Blades for a set time.

DMG

Pinion Blade

Unleashes a powerful forward slash by expending a gauge built up via attacking.

DMG

Jetsweep Jolt

Build up the gauge by attacking or dodging, then expend it to fire a single powerful blast.
