Passives Skills

PP

Overemphasis

Recover PP and HP, as well as temporarily increase max PP for both yourself and other characters in your party.

HP

Resta Field Techter

Increases the amount of health recovered when you use Restasigne, and also heals nearby characters.

HP

United Techniques: Techter

Enables use of Compound Techniques. Fill the Compound Technique Gauge by attacking, then use the gauge to deploy Compound Techniques. Can be stocked up to 2 times.

HP/DPS

Shifta/Deband Party Share

Distant characters in your party will still receive the effect of Shifta/Deband when it is used.

DPS

Shifta Effect Advantage

Shifta/Deband will additionally increase damage to downed enemies.

DPS

Shifta Effect Amplifier

Shifta/Deband will additionally increase enemy Down Factor..

PP

Deband PP Recovery Boost

Shifta/Deband will additionally increase natural PP recovery speed.

HP

Deband Ward Bad Condition

Shifta/Deband will additionally increase status aliment resistance.

HP

Reverse Bounty

Confers Shifta/Deband to a revived ally.

HP/DPS

Shifta/Deband Extension

Extends the effect duration of Shifta/Deband.

HP/DPS

Long Range Shifta Deband

Launches Shifta/Deband forward. Shares the same cooldown time with normal Shifta/Deband.

PP

Talis Bloom After PP Gain

At the end of a Talis deployment triggered by a Photon Art, you recover PP based on the number of times a Technique was used while it was deployed.

DPS

Wand Lovers Line Force

The speed and potency of your normal attacks increases during Wand Lovers.

DPS

Tricky Capacitor Gauge Amplifier

While your Talis is deployed due to a Photon Art, the level of your Tricky Capacitor gauge increases when you attack an enemy's elemental weak point with a Technique.

DPS
