Passive Skills

PP

Dodge PP Gain

A successful dodge using Sidestep or a Twin Machine Gun weapon action recovers PP.

DMG

Rising Drive

At just the right timing after performing a weapon action, pressing the normal attack button will cause you to rise while attacking.

DMG

Chain Trigger Quick Reload

After Chain Finisher Boost has triggered, depending on the size of the Chain during Chain Trigger, the re-use time will be shortened.

DMG

Chain Boost

For a set time after Chain Finisher Boost has triggered, Weapon Actions will change, and attack speed will increase depending on the size of the chain.

DMG

Chain Trigger Count Retain

When a Chain Trigger ends without a Chain Finisher Boost, the Chain Number of the next Chain Trigger you use will start at a higher number based on the previous Chain Number.

DMG

Chain Finish Advance

Makes you invulnerable for a set period of time when you perform a Chain Finish.

DMG

Chain Trigger Increase

Increases the Maximum Chain Number of a Chain Trigger to 150. However, potency increase per each chain will be reduced after Chain 101.

DMG

Chain Boost Advanced

During a Chain Boost, your weapon action's invulnerability is extended. Successful dodges using your weapon action extend the duration of your chain boost.

HP

Strikeback Advanced

After a successful dodge using a Weapon Action, the next Photon Art you use will grant an invulnerability period.

Active Skills

DMG

Hail of Bullets

Performs a sustained rapid-fire attack by expending a gauge built up via attacking. Chain Finish and Chain Boost effects are not added to this attack.

DMG

Spread Shot

Expend a gauge built up by attacks to fire a special close range shotgun blast.
