Passive Skills

DPS

Blight Rounds Reinforce

Parts hit by Blight Rounds that are hit a set number of times will take increased damage, and the effect duration of the skill will be extended.

DPS

Launcher Blight Rounds

Allows you to use Blight Rounds while equipped with a launcher.

DPS

Spread Shot Wide Range

Changes Spread Shot to an area-of-effect attack to your front.

Active Skills

DPS

Blight Rounds

Fires a bullet that increases damage dealt to the hit part.

DPS

Wide Range Blight Rounds

Fires rounds that increases damage dealt to any part hit in a wide area of effect. This skill draws from the same number of uses and cooldown time of ordinary Blight Rounds.

DPS

Spread Shot

Expend a gauge built up by attacks to fire a special close range shotgun blast.

DPS

Launcher Attack Reinforce

Increases the potency and speed of uncharged Normal Attacks for a set time.
