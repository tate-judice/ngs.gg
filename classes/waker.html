Passive Skills

DPS

Marmelo Strike

Negating a certain number of attacks with a Weapon Action will cause Marmelo to grow up to two times. Press and hold the Weapon Action button to throw Marmelo, causing an explosion upon a successful hit.

DPS

Marmelo Strike Tracing

Marmelo Strike gains the ability to home in on enemies.

HP

Marmelo Patience

While you have the Weapon Action button held down, reduces damage received and protects you from being knocked back or launched. In addition, Marmelo's gauge builds when you are hit by an attack.

DPS

Hot Marmelo Parry Short Cooldown

Cooldown time for Hot Marmelo Parry is reduced when you successfully avoid an attack with it.

Active Skills

DPS

Familiar Harmony

Launch a linked attack with Familiars Fredran and Wulfen by expending some of their Harmonizer Focus Gauges.

DPS

Hot Marmelo Parry

Enemy attacks are negated without interrupting your own attack.
