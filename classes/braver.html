Passives Skills

PP

Brave Spirit

Temporarily increases PP recovery when attacking as well as the potency of your normal attacks and counters when you perform a successful perfect guard with a weapon action.

DMG

Special Ability Optimization

The Melee Weapon Potency and Ranged Weapon Potency bonuses from your equipped weapon and units Augments are each added up, and the one that is higher is applied to both your Melee and Ranged Weapon Potency.

DMG

Final Bonus Surprise

Doubles the number of Brave Combat Final Bonus hits when you attack a boss enemy during Brave Combat.

DMG

Brave Combat Cross Spirit

When you trigger Brave Combat, Brave Spirit also becomes active.

PP

Bow Arts Combo PP Save

Chaining Photon Arts together reduces your PP consumption.

PP

Fearless Attitude PP Preservation

Reduces the PP Consumption of the first Photon Art you use after activating Fearless Attitude.

PP

Katana Perfect Guard PP Gain

Recovers PP when you successfully execute a perfect guard.

Active Skills

PP

Brave Combat

Reduces PP consumption and increases attack PP recovery for a fixed time. Using the skill while activated will launch a powerful attack.
