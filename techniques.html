Compound Techniques

There are 3 types of Compound Techniques known as Fomelgion, Barantsion, and Zandion which all draw from the same Compound Technique Gauge. Hitting enemies with Techniques and Photonic Fury will fill the Compound Technique Gauge.
Damage caused by Compound Techniques does not vary based on an enemy's elemental weakness, and Technique class skills have no effect.
